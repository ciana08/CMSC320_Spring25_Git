Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: It allows us to manage different versions of our code when working with a team.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines branches and saves the history while rebasing rewrites the history since it moves the commits to another branch.
